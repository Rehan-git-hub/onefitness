Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

At One Fitness, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or visit our facility at 13 Mosque Road, Bengaluru.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address provided during sign-up or inquiries.
  • Health & Fitness Data: BCA body composition metrics, weight, and general health history to provide personalized training and nutrition advice.
  • Payment Information: Billing details securely processed through our payment gateways (we do not store your raw credit card numbers).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Manage your membership and provide gym access.
  • Communicate updates, gym closures, and special offers.
  • Track your fitness progress (only accessible by you and your designated trainer).
  • Improve our services and facility layout based on member feedback.

4. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your data is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized One Fitness staff members for operational purposes. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, or to request its deletion. If you would like to exercise these rights, please contact management.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the front desk or call 099805 22000.
